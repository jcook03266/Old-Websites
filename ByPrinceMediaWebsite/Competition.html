Pricing
Independent Freelancers
On sites like Fiverr and Upwork



While you see many editors have prices as low as $5, however, quality isn’t assured and you can potentially spend more time finding an editor than getting actual work done.
Companies Hiring One or More Personal Editors


Usually when you hire a personal editor you’re offloading all of your work to maybe one to two people and paying them hourly rates. This doesn’t account for facts like their computer taking too long to render a video or if they unfortunately lose footage due to hardware failure. Too many mishaps can occur when having a personal editor- take it from us.

Notable Competitors

Viddedit.com

  • They do multiple types of videos, ranging from weeding, corporate, vacation, etc.

  • Because they have a range of videos, they also have different ranges of prices depending on the type of video, including add-ons. E.g. the Corporate Video Editing Service option, the default price starts at $80 for uploaded footage up to 30 minutes. If we match it to a weekly podcast video edit Prince Media handles for DealRoom, you would be paying $220 for a 60-minute video. You will be paying extra for every extra 30-minutes in the video, ranging from $40-80. You have to pay for things like photos if you want to add it to the video.

  • Videos for a company like this are usually one and done projects, like wedding edits and ads. Only three revisions are included per project.

    •
    VeedYou Media

  • Similar to Viddedit as they also are able to do multiple types of videos.

  • Their “getting started” option minimizes the options into 3 categories, each in different price points. For the Corporate Video, pricing starts at $99 for footage up to 30 minutes. If we choose a 60-minute video, you’re paying $159, which is significantly cheaper than Viddedit, but still extremely high.

  • No option to upload footage longer than 4 hours, must pay an extra $50 to include photos and another $50 for animated text and titles.

  • Again, only good for one-time video edits.

  • Only three free revisions.
